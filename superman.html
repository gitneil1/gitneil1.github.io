Codename: Superman

Name: Clark Kent/Kal-El

Sex: Male

Age: 25

Location: Metropolis City

Background:
Superman was born to Lara Lor-Van and Jor-El, a famous scientist who had predicted the planet's impending doom. Lara took the baby and fled the city of Kandor upon Jor-El's warnings -- Brainiac was invading. He soon bottled the city. However, Lara and Kal-El got out in time.

Strengths:

  • Super physical strength
  • Super speed
  • Super senses
  • Flight
  • Laser beams from his eyes
  • Super durability
  • Enhanced healing factor
  • Can breathe in outer space

Weaknesses:

  • Exposure to Kryptonite
